The Best Desserts

After any delicious meal, you always need to finish off with an outstanding dessert! But, what should you have? The are many desserts that you could choose from that other would like too. One of the most popular deserts is just a plain old chocloate chip cookie. They have the perfect amount of sweetness and could be the perfect amout, especially after dinner. A few other delicious basic desserts are cakes, pies, brownies, and always top them off with some ice cream!

If you are a little fancier then just some cookies, you could always serve a desert nice and warm so when you add ice cream it will be the perfect temperature. You could always have a variety of different things. For example, instead of just chocolate chip cookies you could add some oatmeal raisen, or M&M cookies. By having a variety of different desserts, you could satisfy more people with the different choices.
